DIRECTOR- ADMINISTRATION MESSAGE

Dear Parents and Students, I am delighted to extend my warmest welcome to you as the Director Administration of Delhi Public School Pilibhit. As we embark on another academic year, I am lled with pride and anticipation for the journey ahead

At Delhi Public School Pilibhit our mission is to provide a world-class education that not only imparts knowledge but also nurtures character, integrity, and a passion for lifelong learning. We believe in empowering our students to be global citizens, equipped to face the challenges of an ever-evolving world.

MANAGEMENT - DPS PILIBHIT EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE Mr. V K Shunglu (Chairman Delhi Public School Pilibhit) Retired Indian Administrative Services Officer. Served as Controller & Auditor General. Serves as the Chairman at ASREC (India) Limited. He represented Govt. of India at International Bodies like Asian Development Bank, IMF/World Bank, International Organization of the Supreme Audit Institutions, Asian Organization of the Supreme Audit Institutions, ASOSAI, etc. Founder member of Shunglu Committee that was formed to examine the alleged irregularities and cases of nepotism in the appointments across various Delhi State Government Departments. Our Esteemed Chairman Mr. V.K. Shunglu is an inspirational force for all the members of D.P.S. Pilibhit. An erudite and an illustrious personality who has inspired us to dream great and believe that there is strength, patience and the passion in each one of us to reach the stars and change the world.

Our dedicated faculty, who are not just teachers but mentors, strive to inspire and guide each student to realize their full potential. Our well-rounded curriculum fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a sense of responsibility, preparing our students for success in the real world.

Education at Delhi Public School Pilibhit extends beyond the classroom. We offer a rich tapestry of co-curricular activities, sports, arts, and community engagement opportunities, ensuring that every student's unique talents and interests nd a place to shine. As parents and students, you become integral members of our school community, and I invite you to be part of this exciting educational journey. Together, let's shape a brighter future for our children. Thank you for considering Delhi Public School Pilibhit as your educational partner. Wishing you a year lled with growth, success, and memorable experiences.
