TECHNOLOGY EMBRACEMENT

At DPS, we believe in absolute technology embracement to enhance the teaching-learning process. Learning becomes easier, more effective and impactful. This is an endeavour to equip the students with the upcoming technological trends, tools and techniques and new-age developments like VR (Virtual Reality) and AR (Augmented Reality). Interactive and seamless lecture delivery through smart class modules. Complete Digital school management and Parent App for effective and instant communication. English language lab to empower the students via a self-learning mode to improve their English communication skills. Mathematical modelling activities using technology making math more enjoyable and participative. 3D printing to help foster creativity and spark student’s imagination. Conceptualising and creating 3d models enhances critical thinking abilities. Robotics to aspire the creativity and logic of the young minds.
